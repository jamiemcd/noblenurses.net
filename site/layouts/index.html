Welcome to Noble Nurses

We provide onsite CPR certification in the Central Kentucky area.

Healthcare providers of all types are required to renew their American Heart Association credentials every two years. Noble Nurses makes this convenient by coming to your home or place of business. Certification courses are also regularly available at our office in Lexington, Kentucky.

Whether you need to obtain or renew your BLS, ACLS, or PALS certifications, Noble Nurses provides training in a relaxed and enjoyable environment. Call today to schedule your next certification course!

Certification at Your Office

For your convenience Noble Nurses will come to your place of business for CPR training. This is a popular service for hospitals, doctor's and dentist's offices, nursing homes, companies, stores, daycares, and churches. A travel fee is added for locations outside of Lexington, Kentucky.

Certification at Our Office

Noble Nurses is located at 2692 Richmond Service Road, Suite 220 in Lexington, Kentucky. You will feel at home in our cozy office space. Our classrooms are setup for individual and group CPR training.
