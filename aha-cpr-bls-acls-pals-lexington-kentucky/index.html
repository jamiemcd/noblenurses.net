American Heart Association Certification

AHA certification courses are our most popular services available. As medical professionals, we consider AHA the industry setting standard.

Noble Nurses ensures you are trained efficiently and effectively by a credentialed professional that is certified as an AHA instructor.

See below to find which AHA certification is right for you. All certifications we offer include CPR training.

We are now offering Blended Learning! With all the uncertainty that Covid has caused, the blended learning format is perfect for those that wish to complete coursework comfortably at home. Once completed, all you need to do is contact Noble Nurses, LLC to schedule your hands-on skills session. We will work with your schedule by manipulating ours to meet your needs. It’s that simple!

HeartCode BLS, ACLS, & PALS are the American Heart Association’s blended learning delivery methods. Blended learning is a combination of eLearning, in which a student completes part of the course in a self-directed manner, followed by a hands-on skills session with a certified AHA instructor.

Pediatric Advanced Life Support

AHA's PALS course uses videos and simulated pediatric emergencies to reinforce the important concepts of a systematic approach to pediatric assessment, basic life support, PALS treatment algorithms, effective resuscitation, and team dynamics.

PEARS

Pediatric Emergency, Assessment, Recognition, & Stabilization

$200 Initial / $150 Renewal

AHA's PEARS course teaches providers who infrequently deal with pediatrics how to use a systematic approach to quickly assess, recognize the cause, and stabilize a pediatric patient in an emergency situation.

Schedule PEARS
Heartsaver

First Aid CPR AED

$135

AHA's Heartsaver First Aid CPR AED course is for everyone, including those with no prior medical training. In this course you will learn to provide first aid, CPR, and use an automated external defibrillator (AED) in a safe, timely, and effective manner.

This course is designed to meet OSHA requirements for first aid CPR AED training.
